INTRODUCTIONS

Hello, everyone! My name is Abraham Gonzalez and I am a Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Ph.D. student at the University of California at Berkeley in the SLICE lab (formerly the ADEPT lab) under Professor Krste Asanovic. I am a highly motivated individual, passionate on learning more about computer microarchitecture and datacenter computing. I hope to make future computing faster, more efficient, and more available to all.
